I was born in 1995 on the central coast of Chile, where I spent most of my youth. I studied Mathematics (engineering, B.Sc. & M.Sc.) at Universidad Técnica Federico Santa María (UTFSM) in Valparaíso under the supervision of Alberto Mercado. Later, I completed my PhD at Institut National des Sciences Appliquées (INSA) in Rouen, with joint supervision by Hasnaa Zidani, Ioana Ciotir & Nicolas Forcadel. I currently work at Alpen-Adria-Universität (AAU) in Klagenfurt in the group of Caroline Geiersbach.
